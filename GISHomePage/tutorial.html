How to get Started

  1. Click the "GIS main page" link below.
  2. Go to the bottom of the page and click create account.
  3. Create your account.
  4. The rest of the tutorial should be followed using your smart phone.
  5. Click the "Download the APP" link below.
  6. Click the GIT_Alpha_v2_8.adk and download it.
  7. Make sure you allow your phone's security to accept the app.
  8. Run the app and type in your username and password you created from the home page.
  9. Be amazed as the app displays your coordinates.
  10. Set how often you would like the app to update your location.
  11. You can now use any computer to login to the website and locate your phone.
