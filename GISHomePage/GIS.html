Welcome to the GIS Web Application

Key Features

Mobile Geolocation

  • Log into the app with Username and Password
  • Your Phone will automatically locate your position
  • It will display your coordinates and add it to the database
  • GIS will continue to run in the background

Interval updates

  • Choose how often you would like to update your coordinates





The Web Page

  • The Phones UUID and most recent Coordinates will be displayed
  • Google maps will show show a map of the coordinates
  • You can add another device to your account









Device settings

  • You will be able to toggle your phones informaiton
  • You can deleate your phone from the account
